Speakers Who Want a Platform

Purpose

Often people say that they cannot find speakers from underrepresented groups, whether it's black people, LGBT individuals, women, or others. This wbesite exists to serve as an easy way to find people from these groups and extend invitations to them to come and speak at your conferences or events.
